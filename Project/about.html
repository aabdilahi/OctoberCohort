What is Luxury Holidays?

We are the experts in bespoke luxury holidays. Luxury is personal to each and every one of our guests, which is why we have always truly tailored our holidays. Since we first started Luxury Holidays in 2023, we have constantly honed and polished our collection. Today, we offer holidays across the world, from the Mediterranean to the Caribbean, as far from the Far East to the Maldives. We spend time handpicking our destinations and selecting partners that we know can guarantee the service and high standards both we and our guests demand. We do not settle for anything less than the best. Luxury Holidays joined an amazing collection of brands when Travelopia took us under their wing in 2023. We look forward to sharing the next 50 years with you.

Why Luxury Holidays?

  • Great Value - We stand for affordable luxury. You'll always receive exceptional experiences at competitive prices. All prices within this brochure include flights, complimentary lounge passes and private transfers as standard.
  • Experienced - We have been crafting luxury holidays for our guests for 50 years and know the industry. We are fully ATOL and ABTA bonded and strictly managed by the Civil Aviation Authority (CAA).
  • Tailored - Your luxury holiday is crafted just for you, based on your interests and needs. Expect personalised service as standard, for a smooth and hassle-free holiday.
  • Exclusive - You will always benefit from exclusive offers and upgrades thanks to our great, long-term partnerships. Enjoy various perks, including complimentary airport lounge access.
  • Expert - You will have a single point of contact with your own assigned Personal Travel Planner. Benefit from our destination expertise, 24/7 support, and up-to-the-minute travel advice.
